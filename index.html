Welcome to the Church of Elohym.
Join our community in search for comfort through our Lady.

We seek to honour our Mother and Her Daughter,
and to live righteously by the Light.




What is our faith?

We believe that Elohym, the Mother created the universe and that She brought about Elith, the Daughter, she who gave us hearts with which to love and to be loved.
By following Her word and way, we can find peace within ourselves and with our fellow kin; And by walking as the Light leads, we shall be righteous and reasonable.

A longer, more elaborate statement of faith can be found on another page.


Join the community

As we do each other, we'll support you in your search for happiness and to feel fullfilled. Come be a part of our community, our family, and together stride with us. We shall find the light at the end of the tunnel; She will guide us there.

As a first step, you can head over to our Facebook page and follow it; then should enough people come along, we can justify opening a group.

Unlike some churches, where you might have been hurt or rejected, we give everyone a chance. If you're of the GRSM ("Gender, Sexual, and Romantic Minorities", or LGBT), you don't need to fear being shunned; you're as welcomed as any.
