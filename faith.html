The Leyvera

The book of Leyvera is the scripture in which we lay our faith. As it is a very old collection of texts, they need to be rewritten into Modern English to be understood.

You can find it as a PDF document.

It's currently incomplete but a preview of the first chapter of the first book will soon be available. Later, the entire Leyvera will be available to download from the website.
